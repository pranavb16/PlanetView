Satellite Image Segmentation

Planet View provides 11 channel segmented images of any location on Earth and further offers assistance with analysis of physical parameters of the region.

Features of the model

High Spacial Resolution

Satellite images offer detailed information about Earth's surface due to their high spatial resolution, enabling the detection of fine-grained features and objects.

Classification Algorithms

Various machine learning algorithms can be employed for satellite image segmentation, including traditional approaches like clustering, as well as more advanced techniques like CNNs can also be used.

Accuracy Assessment

Validating the accuracy of segmented results is crucial. Metrics like overall accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 score are commonly used to evaluate the performance of segmentation algorithms.
